Gula

A simple electronic health records system

Gula is a simple, open source electronic health records system designed to respond to the lack of open source EHR systems that are also simple to setup. Please check out this project by registering to the top-right, or checking our the GitHub page below.



Made with by Gustavo Arellano, Evan Tung, Archana Madhavan, Gayathri Sitaraman, Kito Mam, and Tanvi Thote
