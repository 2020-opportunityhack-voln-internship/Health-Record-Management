Arkay

A simple electronic health records system

Arkay is a simple, open source electronic health records system designed to respond to the lack of open source EHR systems that are also simple to setup. Arkay EHR is both easy to use and follows your workflow, meaning that you can spend less time documenting medical information and more time interacting with your patients. You can check out our GitHub repository below, try out this demonstration by registering to the top-right, or contact us if you have any questions regarding this project.
